About Us

Learn About our Team & Culture.

Frost Gaming labs is talented and willing game studio situated in the east of Europe. We are focused on high-quality HTML5 game development. The team consists of gamers that's why we are so passionate in the processes of development. Our main goal is to make our product entertaining and convenient. Frost Gaming labs studio always works at improving quality of its product. We use the latest solutions in order to achieve a better result.
