Our partners:

Andrzej Mazur is a JavaScript programmer and HTML5 games developer, blogger, speaker, Enclave Games founder, js13kGames creator and Gamedev.js organizer.
Js13kGames is a JavaScript coding competition for HTML5 game developers. The fun part of the compo is the file size limit set to 13 kilobytes.
Gamedev.js is an initiative of a community meetups based around HTML5 games. They are free and open to anyone who wants to learn new, cool things.
onGameStart is the first and biggest HTML5 games development conference organized by Michał Budzyński with many editions in Warsaw and one in New York. The best developers gather and share their knowledge with the community.
BlackMoon Design is an Indie Game Development Studio founded by pixel-artist and graphic designer Robert Podgórski. They are based in Poznań, Poland and their most known production so far is The Few - RTS about Battle of Britain.
